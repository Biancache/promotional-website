Atonement

An epic story of love, struggle, and regret.

In the summer of 1935, young Briony Tallis witnesses a series of events between her older sister, Cecilia, and Robbie Turner, the son of a servant. Misunderstandings and youthful imagination lead Briony to make a false accusation that drastically alters the course of their lives. Directed by Joe Wright and based on the acclaimed novel by Ian McEwan, "Atonement" is a sweeping romantic drama that explores the complexities of love, war, and the profound impact of one's decisions. The narrative unfolds across different timelines, capturing the brutal realities of World War II and the enduring hope of lost love. With powerful performances and evocative cinematography, "Atonement" is a haunting tale of passion, redemption, and the lasting consequences of a single act.
